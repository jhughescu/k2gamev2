Requests for assistance

You will see a head bubble appear on your screen when a climber is in need of assistance. Select the bubble to listen to their radio call.

After the call, you will need to decide how best to assist your climber.

Solving problems will generally incur additional waiting time or use up your climberâ€™s resources.

Generally, you will be able to ask fellow climbers in the same section for assistance with a problem, but they can say no to helping you!

During your climb, you can help other climbers by sharing your resources with them.
